# Domain Glossary

## Companion

Main character linked to the player, with affinity, conversations, personality, visual assets, and possible Myrion links.

## Affinity / Intimacy / Link

Relationship level with a companion. Higher levels should unlock more personal or intimate conversations.

## Myrion

Collectible creature tied to biomes, hunting, capture, refuge, training, passive production, resources, variants, and shiny tracking.

## Biome

Thematic zone influencing Myrions, resources, rarity, variants, combat types, backgrounds, and progression.

## Refuge

Management area for captured Myrions. May include nursery, craft, release, assignments, buffs, and visual wandering.

## Release

Freeing a Myrion in exchange for rewards, biome favor, or progression. Must clean links and update counters correctly.

## Variant

A Myrion variation. Do not confuse with shiny.

## Shiny

Rare or special Myrion form. Tracking must remain separate from variants.

## Biome Favor

Progression or reputation tied to a biome.

## Passive Production

System where assigned Myrions generate resources in places such as farm, mine, forest, dungeon, or workshop.

## Papouillage

Care/training minigame inspired by tactile petting or brushing interactions.

## Racing

Training minigame with tracks, boosts, obstacles, and item boxes.

## Combat

1v1 training/minigame system inspired by creature battles, with biome-linked types and moves.
